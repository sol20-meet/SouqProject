About The Website and Myself

Hello my name is Sol, I created this website as a project for my TA application process, It's a website which is called TravelHub and it gives people the chance to talk about their experiences around the world and share images with others, this helps us to raise awareness about many unknown places around the world.

I want to become a TA so I could give back to this organization as much as I can, the amount of things that Meet has given to me can’t be listed nor can be counted, so I just wanna give out the most out of what I learned throughout my journey in Meet back to the students and to the organization.

I was a TA during 2021’s summer in Meet, and I can say that it was one of the best experiences ever, even though i'm not a student it was still a pleasure to be there and to work with the staff and to interact with the students and work with them, seeing the student’s happiness and excitement once that one small error gets fixed in his project is the main reason why I used to finish a whole day full of stress and a hard schedule filled with sessions with a smile on my face and zero regrets on why I’m not doing anything else during my summer.
